Consent to Participate in a Research Study
ADULT

OBSESSIVE-COMPULSIVE SYMPTOMS

CONCISE SUMMARY

The purpose of this study is to investigate the relation between obsessive-compulsive symptoms and two psychological tasks.

The duration of the study will be about 40 minutes and there is minimal risk associated with participation.

To take part in this study you must be a healthy adult between the ages of 18 and 60. Research studies are voluntary and include only people who choose to take part.

Please read this consent form carefully and take your time making your decision. As you review this document, please feel free to contact the researchers and ask them to explain any words or information that you do not clearly understand.

WHY IS THIS STUDY BEING DONE?

The purpose of this study is to investigate the relation between obsessive-compulsive symptoms (including intolerance of uncertainty), as measured by three questionnaires, and two psychological tasks designed to evaluate performance monitoring and decision making.

HOW MANY PEOPLE WILL TAKE PART IN THIS STUDY?

Approximately 200 people will take part in this online study.

WHAT IS INVOLVED IN THE STUDY?

If you agree to participate, you will answer a number of questions about demographic data and obsessive-compulsive symptoms. This must take around 10 minutes. Then you will respond two psychological tasks. Each one of them shall take around 15 minutes. The order in which the questionnaires and tasks are presented may vary between participants.

WHAT ARE THE RISKS OF THE STUDY?

There are no physical risks associated with this study.

There is a chance you might feel bothered or uncomfortable by some of the questions or tasks. These emotions are unlikely to be stronger than those you would naturally experience in real life, however you may contact the researcher if you are experiencing very uncomfortable emotions or feeling unwell.

ARE THERE BENEFITS TO TAKING PART IN THE STUDY?

If you agree to take part in this study, there is no medical benefit to you. You might have insights about the way you think and behave. And you surely will be helping us to better understand some symptoms of OCD and further help people with this disorder.

WILL MY INFORMATION BE KEPT CONFIDENTIAL?

The study results will be retained in your research record for at least five years after the study is completed. While the information and data resulting from this study may be presented at scientific meetings or published in a scientific journal, no personal information will be revealed.

COMPENSATION

If you complete the survey, you will be paid the amount of money described in the research ad, according to Amazon Mechanical Turk rules.

WHAT ABOUT MY RIGHTS TO DECLINE PARTICIPATION OR WITHDRAW FROM THE STUDY?

You may choose not to be in the study, or, if you agree to be in the study, you may withdraw from the study at any time. If you withdraw from the study, no new data about you will be collected for study purposes other than data needed to keep track of your withdrawal.
